ABOUT US

PatronGG is an eSports startup made up of esports enthusiasts, backed by esports verterns with the goal of providing fans witha central placeto follow their favourite team(s) and players, take part in discussions and compete to be the #1 fan.

Our first app "Fnatic Nation" was built as the offical app for Fnatic to help them grow, retain and monetize their fanbase. Now, we're focusing on uniting fans and teams in one place.
